Common questions

Things People Ask Before They Work With Us

If your question isn't here, just ask. We reply fast.

How much does it cost?

Every project is scoped individually because every business is different — a simple automation and a full multi-user platform aren't the same thing. What we promise is complete transparency: you'll have a clear, itemised quote before any commitment. Projects typically range from a few hundred pounds for targeted automations to £5,000–£20,000+ for full custom platforms. And remember — with our Proof-First approach, you see a working prototype before you spend anything.

How long does a build take?

Most focused automations and tools are live in 1–3 weeks. Full custom web apps with auth, databases, and integrations typically take 3–8 weeks depending on complexity. We work fast and we communicate constantly — you're never in the dark about where things are. We'd rather tell you it takes a bit longer than promise something and miss it.

Can it actually be AI-powered?

Yes — and it's one of our biggest strengths. We bring AI into builds wherever it genuinely adds value: document understanding, intelligent categorisation, natural language interfaces, predictive logic, content generation, and more. We also build human-in-the-loop systems where AI does the heavy lifting and a human makes the final call — the best of both worlds.

Are the systems secure?

Absolutely. Security isn't an afterthought — it's built in from day one. We use industry-standard auth (encrypted credentials, role-based access, session management), encrypted data at rest and in transit, and follow OWASP security standards throughout. For regulated industries like healthcare or finance, we're happy to discuss specific compliance requirements.

Can you automate our marketing?

Yes. We've built automated email sequences, social media scheduling pipelines, lead scoring systems, CRM automation, and AI-generated content workflows. If there's a repetitive marketing task your team does manually, there's a very good chance we can automate most of it. Book a call and we'll tell you honestly what's feasible.

Can you build a system with multiple users and different roles?

Multi-user systems with role-based access are what we do. Managers, admins, staff, clients — everyone sees exactly what they need and nothing they don't. We handle authentication, permissions, audit trails, and all the complexity that comes with it. It's standard in our builds.

Can you build a website, mobile app, or data-heavy system?

All three, yes. Websites and web apps are our core. Mobile-ready progressive web apps are part of our standard toolkit. For data-heavy systems — large datasets, complex reporting, analytics dashboards — we have specific experience and enjoy those challenges. If you need a native mobile app (iOS/Android), we can discuss what makes the most sense for your budget and use case.

What if I don't know exactly what I need?

That's completely fine — and honestly, quite common. The discovery call is specifically designed for this. Bring us your pain points and goals. We'll ask questions, understand your business, and suggest what to build. You might come in thinking you need a new website and leave with a plan for an automated workflow that saves your team 20 hours a week. We're not here to upsell — we're here to solve the actual problem.

Can you replace tools like Zapier, Notion, or other SaaS subscriptions?

Often, yes. Many businesses are paying £500–£5,000+ per month for a collection of SaaS tools that don't quite do what they need. A bespoke system can replace multiple subscriptions, work exactly how your business works (not how the software vendor designed it), and costs you a one-time build rather than an endless monthly fee. The ROI is often immediate.

What does ongoing support look like?

After launch, we offer ongoing partnership options — monthly retainers for continued development, maintenance, and feature additions. Most of our clients stay with us long-term because they see the compounding value of having a team that knows their system and keeps improving it. We'll always be honest if we think a simpler (or cheaper) arrangement is better for your stage.
